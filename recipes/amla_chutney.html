Hereby is the recipe to make 1 cup of chutney

Ingredients

  • 2 amlas
  • 1 buch coriander
  • rock salt
  • 1 Green chillie
  • chat masala
  • cumin

    • Directions

    • Mix coriander leaves, mint leaves ,green chilli and amlas
    • Add 1/4 th teaspoon chat masala and 1/3rd teaspoon of roasted cumin to it
    • Add rock salt according to the taste
    • Grind the mixture
    • Kudos!!Serve it with food.
